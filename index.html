Rocket Speed Service

The primary goal of this application is to ensure that there is no overcrowding and allowing the users to visit places at their convenience without putting themselves at risk.

The users will be notified if the stock they are looking for is available or not saving them time and decreasing the exposure by not having to wait. The service providers can make sure the stock is available depending on the number of slots booked.

Increased Productivity

Finding what you are looking for at the right stop is a blessing in disguise. This also helps in saving your time and energy one puts in getting the things they want, hence increasing productivity. The service providers can keep their stores running efficiently and effectively, which boosts their sales. The users can book a slot, pick their items, and make a move.

Pricing

It is a free service offered to the users solely concentrating on user safety and security. Because we believe there shouldn’t be a price on our user’s safety and our fight against the virus. .
